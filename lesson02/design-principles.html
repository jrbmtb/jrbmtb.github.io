Repetition

Invicta Watch Group

Website: InvictaWatch.com

Invicta has always identified its brand with the color yellow for advertisement, design, and even for its products. In its webpage, you can notice how they use the principle of repetition by using the yellow tone that characterizes the brand so well on plenty of elements like the logo, pictures, buttons, and menus.

Balance

Zara España

Website: Zara.com

Zara España, a fashion company, is a great example of balance. The models are centered on each photo, which gives structure to the page, giving the feeling that the pictures are acting as pillars, supporting other content like text and headings, a pattern that is pleasing for the viewers.

Proximity

Red Bull

Website: RedBull.com

RedBull is a worldwide known company, famous for its incredible projects and divisions that always go beyond what is expected. A company like this needs a very well constructed website, and they are not the exception. Everything is well organized; it is easy to distinguish between each article, and it is easy to understand which image belongs to which section. Also, there is a good space between each article to separate them.
